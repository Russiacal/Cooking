Part of a Greek-leaning dinner alongside herb-crusted leg of lamb and new potatoes with herb vinaigrette.

Split verdict. The whipped ricotta itself was great and I'd do that again. But the grapes kind of wilted and shriveled so they had a more raisin-like texture versus a grilled profile. I had an Ottolenghi recipe that used grilled grapes and was surprised at how interesting the char on them tasted — that's what I was going for here, and oven-roasting didn't get me there.

## Ingredients
- Whole milk ricotta
- Lemon zest
- Olive oil
- Red grapes
- Fresh thyme
- Walnuts
- Honey (to finish)
- Fleur de sel
