## Modifications
- Halved the sugar in the glaze — full amount was cloying last time.
- Broiled 2 extra minutes for a proper crust.
- Used sockeye instead of king because that's what was at the market.

## What worked
- 45-minute marinade hit the sweet spot — the miso really penetrated without turning the fish mushy.
- Rice vinegar in the glaze balanced the sweetness nicely.

## What didn't
- Started the rice too late. Salmon was done and resting for 8 minutes before the rice caught up.
- Next time: rice on first, then everything else.

## Notes
- Would pair well with quick-pickled cucumbers. Try that next round.
