## Modifications
- Used tonnarelli instead of spaghetti — worth the effort of finding it.
- Toasted the black pepper in a dry pan before adding — huge flavor difference.

## What worked
- Pepper toast idea. Doing that from now on.
- Reserved a full mug of pasta water; ended up needing most of it to loosen the sauce.

## What didn't
- Sauce broke when I moved off heat too fast. Cheese seized into clumps.
- Next time: let pan cool a beat longer before adding pecorino, and add it in *three* stages instead of two.

## Notes
- Two servings from 6 oz pasta was a stretch. Bump to 8 oz next round.
