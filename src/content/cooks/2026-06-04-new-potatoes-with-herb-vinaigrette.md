Part of a Greek-leaning dinner alongside herb-crusted leg of lamb and whipped ricotta with roasted grapes.

Loved these. Much fresher taste than the usual olive oil and garlic, and the vinegar, mustard, and capers combo packs more of a punch and just had more flavor all around. Cut right through the fat of the lamb.

## Ingredients
- New potatoes
- Dijon mustard
- White wine vinegar or lemon vinegar
- Capers
- Olive oil
- Fresh dill, parsley, chives
- Spring onions, thinly sliced
- Fleur de sel, black pepper
