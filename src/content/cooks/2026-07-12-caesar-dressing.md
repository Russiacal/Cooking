Super quick Caesar salad dressing — great for a kale Caesar or for dipping veg. I had an open can of anchovies to use up, so this was a good excuse to make it. Mostly pantry ingredients except for the Worcestershire sauce. I ran the immersion blender over everything before adding the mayo, so I skipped the chopping and just threw it all in. Using mayo instead of the egg-and-oil emulsion is a really useful shortcut — I try to use Sir Kensington's.

## Ingredients

- 2 small cloves garlic
- 6–8 anchovy fillets from a can, about half a tin *(in place of 1 tsp anchovy paste)*
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 cup mayonnaise *(Sir Kensington's)*
- ½ cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
- ¼ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper

*Makes about 1⅓ cups · ~10 minutes*
