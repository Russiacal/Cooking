## Modifications
- Higher hydration (78% vs the recipe's 72%) — got a much more open crumb.
- Cold retard in the fridge overnight, baked in the morning at 500°F for 20 min covered + 20 min uncovered.

## What worked
- The crumb was gorgeous — biggest holes I've gotten from this starter.
- Scoring at a shallower angle gave a much better ear.

## What didn't
- Undersalted slightly. Bumping to 2.2% next bake.
- Bottom crust got too dark; try baking on a lower rack.

## Notes
- Starter was 6 hours past peak when I mixed. Might explain the slightly slack dough.
